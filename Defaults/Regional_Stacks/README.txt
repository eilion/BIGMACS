This folder contains benthic d18O stacks that can be used for d18O alignment targets. 
We strongly recommend that cores be aligned to REGIONAL, rather than global, stacks
so that the stack and aligned core share the same benthic d18O signal.

Regional stacks from Lee and Rand (Climate of the Past Discussions, 2022)
DNEA: Deep Northeast Atlantic, 0-150 ka (core depths 2273-3166 m)
ITWA: Intermediate Tropical West Atlantic, 0-60 ka (core depths 1100-1367 m)

Regional and global stacks from Lisiecki and Stern (Paleoceangraphy, 2016)
DI: Deep Indian, 0-145 ka
DNA: Deep North Atlantic, 0-150 ka
DP: Deep Pacific, 0-150 ka
DSA: Deep South Atlantic, 0-150 ka
GLOBAL: Volume-weighted mean of regional stacks, 0-150 ka
II: Intermediate-depth Indian, 0-145 ka
INA: Intermediate North Atlantic, 0-150 ka
IP: Intermediate Pacific, 0-145 ka
ISA: Intermediate South Atlantic, 0-140 ka

Global stack from Lisiecki and Raymo (Paleoceanography, 2005)
LR04: Global, 0-5320 kyr

Global stack from Ahn et al (Dynamics and Statistics of the Climate System, 2017) doi:10.1093/climsys/dzx002
Prob-Stack: Global, 0-5000 kyr
